About Me

Jaighden Rayment

I am a young artist starting up a business to showcase my passion for art. From acrylic and oil-paint,to scratchboards and pastels, I can do it all. I also love to paint beautiful landscapes and still-life. Alongside this business, I was born and raised inPowell, Wyoming and had recently moved to Oahu. I am currently a college student at KCC and work part-time in the Hawaii Army National Guard.
